SalMoN




Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • 1 lemon (sliced)

  • Salt and pepper, to taste

  • Fresh herbs (e.g., dill or parsley)




Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the salmon: Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and place lemon slices on top.
  3. Bake: Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  4. Serve: Garnish with fresh herbs and serve with your favorite side dishes.
