CuPcAkE




Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1/2 cup sugar

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened

  • 2 eggs

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder

  • 1/4 cup milk

  • A pinch of salt




Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven:Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a cupcake tin with liners.
  2. Make the batter: Cream together butter and sugar. Add eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla extract. Gradually mix in the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add milk and mix until smooth.
  3. Bake: Fill the cupcake liners about 2/3 full with batter. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  4. Cool and frost: Let the cupcakes cool completely before frosting with buttercream or your favorite frosting.
