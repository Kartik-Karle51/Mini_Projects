PizZA




Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

  • Toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, bell peppers)

  • Olive oil for brushing



Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven:Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare the dough: Roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness. Transfer to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Add toppings: Spread pizza sauce over the dough. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and arrange your chosen toppings.
  4. Bake: Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Finish :Drizzle with olive oil before serving.
