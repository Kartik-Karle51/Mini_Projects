PaNeeR




Ingredients:

  • 2 cups full-fat milk

  • 2 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar

  • A pinch of salt



Instructions:

  1. Boil the milk: Bring the milk to a boil in a pan. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat.
  2. Curdle the milk: Add lemon juice or vinegar and stir gently. The milk will curdle, and the whey will separate. Turn off the heat.
  3. Strain the curds: Pour the mixture through a cheesecloth or muslin cloth to strain the curds from the whey. Press gently to remove excess water.
  4. Shape the paneer: Tie the cloth and press the paneer under a heavy object for 30 minutes to form a firm block.
  5. Cut and serve: Once set, cut into cubes. Paneer is now ready for use in curries or grilling.
