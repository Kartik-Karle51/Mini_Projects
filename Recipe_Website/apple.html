AppLE PiE




Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

  • 3/4 cup sugar

  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour

  • 4-5 medium apples, peeled and sliced

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 1 package pie crust (store-bought) or homemade crust




Instructions:

  1. Prepare the filling:In a large bowl, toss the apple slices with lemon juice, sugar, cinnamon, flour, and vanilla extract.
  2. Assemble the pie: Line a pie dish with one pie crust. Add the apple mixture, and cover with the second crust. Seal the edges and cut slits in the top for venting.
  3. Bake: Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Bake the pie for 40-45 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  4. Cool and Serve: Let the pie cool before slicing.
