Gulab Jamun




Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1/4 tsp baking soda

  • 2 tbsp ghee

  • 1/4 cup milk

  • 1 tsp cardamom powder

  • Ghee or oil for frying




Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, flour, and baking soda. Add ghee and mix until crumbly. Gradually add milk to form a soft dough. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. For the syrup: Combine sugar, water, and cardamom in a pan, boil until the sugar dissolves. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Shape the dough into small balls. Fry them in hot ghee or oil until golden brown.
  4. Place the hot gulab jamun into the warm sugar syrup and let it soak for at least 30 minutes.
